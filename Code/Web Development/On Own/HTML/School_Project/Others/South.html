Welcome to South Kolkata

South West Kolkata is a locality in Kolkata, India. It is bounded by Tollygunge in the East, New Alipore and Taratala in the North, Sarsuna in the West and extends up to Joka in the South. It includes the localities of Parnasree, Behala, Barisha, Thakurpukur, Sarsuna, Joka, Diamond Park, Sodepur, Haridevpur, Siriti, Sahapur etc.
